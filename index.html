Question 1 of 3
What is Artificial Intelligence?
✅ AI is software that learns from data. It enables machines to simulate human thinking — understanding language, recognising patterns, making decisions. Our policy covers LLMs like Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, image tools, coding assistants, and analytics platforms.
Question 2 of 3
How long has AI actually been around?
✅ AI has been around since the 1950s! Alan Turing asked "Can machines think?" in 1950. What's different now: massive data, cheap computing, and breakthrough models. ChatGPT's 100M users in 2 months wasn't the birth of AI — it was AI going mainstream.

AI 101

AI Didn't Start Yesterday

1950s
Alan Turing proposes the "Turing Test". AI is born as a field of computer science.
1980s
Expert systems and early machine learning emerge from research labs.
2012
Deep learning revolution — machines begin beating humans at image recognition.
2017
Google's "Transformer" architecture lays the foundation for modern AI models.
2022
ChatGPT launches. 100M users in 2 months. AI enters everyday life.
2026 →
Where we are. AI is embedded in every tool, workflow, and industry — including ours.

Section 5 — Don't Drop the Bar

Never Upload This Data

"If you wouldn't post it on a public noticeboard, do not upload it to an UNAPPROVED AI tool."

🚫 Prohibited Data — Ever
Customer personal data — names, emails, purchase history, behavioural data (GDPR applies)
Employee personal data — HR records, salary, performance reviews, health data
Unreleased product designs, launch plans, pricing strategies, supplier terms
Financial information — revenue, margins, fundraising, investor details
Legal documents, contracts, litigation materials, regulatory correspondence
M&A plans, partnership negotiations, business development pipelines
Login credentials, API keys, or any authentication data
✅ Safe to Use
Publicly available information and research
Fully anonymised or synthetic datasets
Your own draft content and creative briefs
Internal process docs with no sensitive data
Approved tools only — enterprise tier with DPA in place
Section 5.3 — Other Prohibited Uses

Other Prohibited Uses

  • 🚫Using AI to generate content that is defamatory, discriminatory, harassing, or in violation of our Code of Conduct
  • 🚫Impersonating another person — colleagues, executives, or public figures
  • 🚫Relying on AI outputs alone for legal, medical, financial, or HR decisions
  • 🚫Using AI to engage in any activity that would violate applicable law
  • 🚫Presenting AI-generated content as entirely your own original work where attribution matters
  • ⚠️Remember: "The AI told me to" is not a defence. You remain fully accountable for all outputs.
Section 6.3 — Data Incidents

If Something Goes Wrong

Accidentally uploaded sensitive data? Here's what to do — immediately.

  • 1Stop using the tool immediately
  • 2Report to IT within 2 hours of discovery — Mohamed.sharukh@squatwolf.com
  • 3Provide a full description of what was uploaded and to which platform
  • 4Cooperate fully with any investigation or remediation steps

⚠️ Failure to report promptly is itself a disciplinary matter — separate from the original breach.
